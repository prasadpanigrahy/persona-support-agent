Basic Interface and Browser Optimization Guide
1. If pages are failing to load on the application interface, it is usually caused by an overwritten local session state.
2. Guide to Clear Cookies: Navigate to your browser's top-right settings icon (three dots), choose 'Clear Browsing Data', toggle the checkbox for 'Cookies and other site data', and select 'Clear Data'. 
3. Refresh the web interface tab to force-load the active production application layout.
